About me!

Hello! My name is Kim Estoesta and I am a senior in the Graphic Design program at the University of Florida. I am an illustrator and designer that plays with art, storytelling, and technology. I like to balance my passion to imagine, create, and design with my love for technology, video, and gaming.

In my spare time I enjoy eating, playing flag football, riding my long board, and making frequent visits to Disney World. I still believe in magic and think that some of the best ideas come from never growing up.

Want to hear more fun facts about me? Think I'm really cool? Don't hesitate to contact me at kimestoesta@gmail.com!
